Creating a Vocal Rubric

UX Design & Standardization for District IX VMEA

Problem

Teachers were frustrated that students were not receiving appropriate feedback on auditions. Adjudicators often contradicted each other. The audition form needed amending to improve the experience for both adjudicators and singers.

Solution

I created a proposed vocal adjudication rubric. This was approved for use, though ultimately changes to the event structure migrated the process online.

Methodologies

Interviews

I conducted user interviews with 6 teachers and adjudicators to understand their pain points. Key finding: Adjudicators needed to communicate a lot of information in very little time (30-90 seconds).

Card Sorting

I performed qualitative analysis of interview notes to find intuitive groupings. Patterns emerged showing that appropriate feedback was the highest priority, but veteran teachers resisted complex changes due to time constraints.

Lo-Fi Prototyping

Using Google Suite, I created low-fidelity prototypes based on the constraints. These prototypes were iteratively refined to ensure they met the primary goal of speed and clarity.
