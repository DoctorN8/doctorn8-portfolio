I'm Nathan St. Pierre, Ph.D., an AI Solutions Architect specializing in building intelligent systems using RAG architecture, prompt engineering, and LLM integration. I create custom AI solutions that solve complex problems in education, nonprofit management, and specialized knowledge domains.

My background in education and research gives me unique insights into creating AI systems that work for real users. I've developed AI-powered chatbots for real estate certification exams, built RAG-based learning systems for educational content, and created specialized AI assistants for Masonic law and nonprofit management.

Before focusing on AI solutions, I spent years as a researcher and educator, with work published in peer-reviewed journals like Update: Applications of Research in Music Education. I've collaborated with organizations like MusicLink Foundation to evaluate program impact and improve educational access for underserved communities. This foundation in research and education informs how I approach AI development—always focused on practical applications that serve real human needs.

Key areas of expertise:
  • RAG Architecture & Implementation
  • Prompt Engineering & LLM Integration
  • Custom AI Chatbot Development
  • Vector Embeddings & FAISS
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Python Development (PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Pandas)
  • Educational Technology & Assessment
  • Research Design & Statistical Analysis

I'm passionate about making advanced AI technology accessible and practical. Whether you need a custom chatbot, a RAG-based knowledge system, or AI integration for your organization, I'd love to connect and explore how AI can solve your challenges.
